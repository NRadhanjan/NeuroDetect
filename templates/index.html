Understanding Autism

What is Autism Spectrum Disorder?

ASD is a developmental condition that affects communication, behavior, and social interaction. Early detection — before age 3 — significantly improves outcomes.

👁️

Social Signs

  • Avoids eye contact
  • Doesn't respond to name
  • Prefers to play alone
💬

Communication Signs

  • Delayed speech development
  • Repeats words or phrases
  • Doesn't point to objects
🔁

Behavioral Signs

  • Repetitive movements
  • Strong need for routine
  • Unusual reactions to senses
